Laughtrack is a comedy show discovery app. Sign-in is optional — all browsing (comedians, shows, clubs, location-based discovery) works without an account. Sign-in (Apple or Google) is only required to favorite comedians and to add shows to the device calendar.

The app is intentionally dark-mode-only for this release. It forces a dark appearance by design, regardless of the device's current light or dark mode setting.

To test full functionality:

1. Open the app — the Near Me tab requests location permission for nearby show discovery. If denied, browsing still works via manual search.
2. Tap the Search tab to browse comedians, shows, and clubs by name. Use the filter controls to narrow by date or distance.
3. Tap any comedian or show to view a detail page. Show detail pages include a ticket link that opens the venue's official ticketing page in Safari.
4. Sign in (Apple or Google, top-right of the Profile tab) to favorite a comedian and save them to the Favorites tab.
5. On a show detail page, the Add to Calendar action requests calendar permission and adds an event with the show date, time, venue address, and ticket URL.

Comedy show data is sourced from comedy club websites and updated nightly. The app does not handle ticket purchases — all ticketing is delegated to the venue's website.
