# LaughTrack App Icon

## Brief

The LaughTrack app icon should feel like the rest of the product brand: warm, theatrical, premium, and comedy-first without drifting into generic podcast branding. The visual anchor is a vintage stage microphone rendered in the app palette of cream, espresso, and copper.

## Visual cues

- Keep one centered hero object with strong small-size readability.
- Use a rich espresso background with subtle spotlight depth.
- Preserve the copper microphone as the primary brand mark.
- Favor clean silhouette and restrained dimensional polish over busy detail.

## Avoid

- Nested tiles or framed cards inside the icon.
- Text, letters, mascots, or cartoon comedy faces.
- Cool neon palettes or generic creator/podcast aesthetics.

## Deliverables

- Cleaned 1024x1024 master icon for iOS and web exports.
- Full iOS `AppIcon.appiconset` generated from the master.
- Matching web icon exports for favicon, Apple touch icon, and PWA sizes.

## Concept review

Three concept directions were explored around the same LaughTrack brand core before selecting the final mark:

- Microphone mark with subtle smile integration: strongest balance of comedy signal, premium feel, and small-size recognition.
- Spotlight or marquee-inspired emblem: warmer and more abstract, but less immediately tied to stand-up comedy.
- Original rounded-tile microphone render: visually strong, but the nested tile treatment would have created a double-framed look on iOS.

## Selected direction

The final icon keeps the microphone as the hero symbol and moves to a full-bleed espresso background so the icon works cleanly with Apple's icon masking. The copper microphone remains readable at small sizes, and the smile cue adds brand personality without turning the mark into a mascot.

## Output locations

- iOS master and icon set: `ios/Resources/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/`
- Web icons: `apps/web/public/icons/`
- Web favicon: `apps/web/public/favicon.ico`
